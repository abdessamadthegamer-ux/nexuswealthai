At NexusWealthAI ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our business intelligence services and website.

Your Trust Matters: We understand that you're sharing sensitive business information with us. This policy outlines our commitment to keeping that information secure and using it only for the purposes you've authorized.

Personal Information

When you request our business analysis, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email address)

  • Business metrics (revenue range, team size, marketing spend)
  • Business challenges and operational information

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide your personalized business analysis and recommendations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Email delivery and communication services

Business Partners

With your explicit consent, we may share your information with qualified marketing agencies and business service providers who can help implement our recommendations.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulations.

Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
